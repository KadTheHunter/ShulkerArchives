This update consists entirely of entries from my backlog of worlds, containing things I've collected or been given since starting the Archives, but had yet to sort through and add. 

This is Part 1 of the backlog, containing entries from various plots and builds I ripped from a server, plus a world sent in by a user.

All the source worlds will be provided on this website in a future update, once I've finished looking through them.
